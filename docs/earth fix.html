earth 2.0

These are my ideas for making a vessel that could theoretically take humanity from earth to a planet that could hold us if and when it becomes the case that it may be necessary. The vessel its self is a sphere with multiple hemispherical layers within it , each spinning at relativistic speeds, as to keep an illusion of 1g of gravity as to curve bone mass loss and other negative effects of micro g,there is a tube through the center in which the control center and all governmentally run labs should be hosted, also were in most food should be produced by government and private entities, on the layers private citizens would be able to own property for homes and private businesses. Also within these rings there would be borders so nations can continue to be a thing so the hassle that would come with taking nations apart can be avoided if there is such a thing at the time of completion, the vessel its self would have 2 propulsion systems a Alcubierre warp drive or something similar capable of similar speeds (90 - 95% of c) and a set of rocket engines to navigate through (relatively) small spaces (small compared to the size earth 2.0 would have to be for example navigating between to planets etc.) I sketch down my ideas and transfer them to electronic forms I have sorted them by category and put them below, again these are ideas and are not ment to thought of anything more, all I am is a amateur putting his ideas out there for all to see because I would like to hope that they may inspire others and come in some use at some point.
