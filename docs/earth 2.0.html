earth 2.0

These are my ideas for making a vessel that could theoretically carry humanity into space,keeping use safe, if and when, it becomes the case that it may be necessary to take such steps. The vessel its self is a sphere with multiple hemispherical layers within it, each spinning at relativistic speeds, with the goal of keeping the illusion of 1g of gravity, as to curve bone mass loss and other negative effects of micro g. There is a tube through the center of the hemispheres in which the control center and other spaces necessary to the proper functioning of the hemispheres lie. On the layers private citizens would be able to own property for homes and private businesses. Also within these rings there would be borders so nations can continue existing assuming there is such a concept at the time of completion. The vessel its self would have 2 propulsion systems a Alcubierre warp drive or something similar capable of similar speeds (90 - 95% of c) and a set of rocket engines to navigate through (relatively) small spaces (for example navigating between to planets etc.).
